# 身份危机五阶段 - 流程图

Professional hand-drawn sketchnote flowchart on pure white background. Black ink line work with slight wobble, à la Mike Rohde sketchnoting.

## STICKY NOTE STYLE
Five small sticky notes arranged in a flow, each representing a stage. Colors progress from neutral (yellow) to dark (gray) to bright (green), symbolizing emotional journey.

## LAYOUT: Horizontal Flow (Left to Right)

## STAGES

### Stage 1: 否认期 (Yellow sticky note #FFF9C4)
- Thought bubble: "AI写的代码质量不行，还是得靠人"
- Stick figure: arms crossed, skeptical expression
- Arrow pointing right

### Stage 2: 愤怒期 (Light orange sticky note #FFE0B2)
- Thought bubble: "这不公平，我花了这么多年学的东西"
- Stick figure: fists raised, frustrated
- Arrow pointing right

### Stage 3: 讨价还价期 (Light pink sticky note #F8BBD9)
- Thought bubble: "我用AI辅助就好了，核心逻辑还是我来"
- Stick figure: hand on chin, negotiating posture
- Arrow pointing right

### Stage 4: 抑郁期 (Gray sticky note #E0E0E0) ⚠️
- Thought bubble: "我到底还有什么价值？"
- Stick figure: slumped shoulders, downcast
- Label: "⚠️ 最危险的阶段"
- Arrow pointing right

### Stage 5: 接受期 (Light green sticky note #C8E6C9)
- Thought bubble: "我的价值不在于写代码，而在于决定写什么代码"
- Stick figure: standing tall, confident
- Star icon: "✓ 成长"

## BOTTOM TAGLINE
Hand-lettered on main green sticky note: "这是正常的，你不是唯一一个"

## COLORS (mono-ink palette with emotional progression)
- Pure White background (#FFFFFF)
- Near Black (#1A1A1A) for all lines, text
- Sticky note colors progress: Yellow → Orange → Pink → Gray → Green
- Coral Red (#E8655A) for ⚠️ warning at Stage 4
- Muted Teal (#5FA8A8) for ✓ success at Stage 5

## ELEMENTS
- Five sticky notes in a row
- Wavy hand-drawn arrows between stages
- Stick figures with different emotional postures
- Thought bubbles with stage-specific thoughts
- Small emotion icons per stage

## STYLE
Hand-lettered stage numbers and titles, handwritten thought content, generous white space. The journey shows emotional progression through sticky note colors.
