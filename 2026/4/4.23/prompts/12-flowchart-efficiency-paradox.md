# 效率悖论三阶段 - 流程图

Professional hand-drawn sketchnote flowchart on pure white background. Black ink line work with slight wobble, à la Mike Rohde sketchnoting.

## STICKY NOTE STYLE
Three sticky notes arranged horizontally showing progression. Colors: Light yellow → Medium yellow → Gray (warning).

## LAYOUT: Horizontal Timeline Flow (Left to Right)

## STAGE 1: 第一阶段 (Light yellow sticky note #FFFDE7)
**时间**: 现在
**状态**: 少数人用 AI
**结果**: 效率差异巨大
**结论**: 用 AI 的人有显著优势 ✓

Icon: One person with AI robot, standing tall

Arrow pointing right →

## STAGE 2: 第二阶段 (Medium yellow sticky note #FFF9C4)
**时间**: 6-12 个月后
**状态**: 多数人开始用 AI
**结果**: 效率差异缩小
**结论**: 仅靠"会用 AI"不再够 ⚠️

Icon: Multiple people with AI robots, similar heights

Arrow pointing right →

## STAGE 3: 第三阶段 (Gray sticky note #E0E0E0)
**时间**: 1-2 年后
**状态**: AI 使用成为基本要求
**结果**: 就像"会用搜索引擎"
**结论**: 效率护城河完全消失 ✗

Icon: Everyone has AI, no differentiation

## BOTTOM CALLOUT (Pink sticky note)
"效率护城河是必要的，但有保质期"
"它给你争取了 6-12 个月的时间窗口"

## KEY INSIGHT BOX
"如果你在效率层停住了——'我已经很会用 AI 了，很厉害'——你就会在第二阶段被淹没"

## COLORS (mono-ink palette with stage progression)
- Pure White background (#FFFFFF)
- Near Black (#1A1A1A) for all lines, text
- Light Yellow (#FFFDE7) for Stage 1
- Medium Yellow (#FFF9C4) for Stage 2
- Gray (#E0E0E0) for Stage 3
- Coral Red (#E8655A) for warning text
- Muted Teal (#5FA8A8) for Stage 1 success

## ELEMENTS
- Three sticky notes in a row
- Wavy hand-drawn arrows between stages
- Clock/calendar icons showing time progression
- Stick figures showing differentiation → similarity
- Warning callout at bottom

## STYLE
Hand-lettered stage numbers and titles, handwritten insights, generous white space. The progression from color to gray shows the "fading advantage" visually.
