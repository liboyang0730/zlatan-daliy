# 金字塔→钻石模型 - 组织变革图

Professional hand-drawn sketchnote framework on pure white background. Black ink line work with slight wobble, à la Mike Rohde sketchnoting.

## STICKY NOTE STYLE
Main content on a large pink sticky note (#FCE4EC, slight shadow, -3-degree tilt). The transformation arrow connects two organizational models.

## LAYOUT: Left-Right Transformation

## LEFT SIDE - 旧模型（金字塔）
On a pale yellow sticky note (#FFFDE7):
```
        P9        ← 1个方向决策者
       P8 P8      ← 2-3个执行管理者
      P7 P7 P7    ← 5-8个骨干
     P6 P6 P6 P6  ← 大量执行者
    P5 P5 P5 P5 P5
```
Label: "金字塔结构"

## RIGHT SIDE - 新模型（钻石型）
On a pale blue sticky note (#E3F2FD):
```
         P9         ← 1个方向决策者
       P8  P8       ← 2个AI+人系统设计者
      P7 P7 P7      ← 核心层：定义问题、驾驭AI
       P6  P6       ← 少量：AI无法替代的专精者
         AI         ← 大量执行工作由AI完成
```
Label: "钻石型结构"

## CENTER ARROW
Large hand-drawn arrow from left to right with label: "AI 时代转型"

## KEY INSIGHT BOX
Small pink sticky note at bottom: "⚠️ P5 这一层直接消失了"

## COLORS (mono-ink palette with sticky note colors)
- Pure White background (#FFFFFF)

- Two pyramid/diamond diagrams on separate sticky notes
- Hand-drawn organizational hierarchy boxes

Hand-lettered headings, handwritten labels, generous white space. Sticky notes appear pinned to whiteboard. Organizational levels shown as stacked rounded rectangles.
